- ActionBarTargeting: http://code.google.com/p/wow5box/wiki/ActionBarTargeting
- MultiboxRoster: http://code.google.com/p/wow5box/wiki/MultiboxRoster

http://phil.freehackers.org/programs/luaunit/index.html
